病史(Brief History) 
This 61 year old man lives alone and has history of type 2 diabetes mellitus. This time, he suffered from fever for about 4-5 days. It accompanied with cough with sputum production and intermittent consciousness disturbance. The symptoms became more and more prominent gradually. Therefor, he went to 永和耕莘醫院. Pneumonia with sepsis was diagnosed there. Because low blood pressure was noted, he was transferred to 博仁綜合醫院. 

    At 博仁醫院 ER, Leukopenia (3/2 WBC: 730, Seg: 57.5%, Lym: 32.9%) with bilateral pulmonary infiltration were noted. Under the impression of community acquired pneumonia with suspected septic shock, he was admitted to the general ward of 博仁醫院 on 3/2 for further treatment and care. After admission, empirical Abx with Tazocin and oral Levofloxacin (500mg QD) were prescribed for severe CAP with septic shock. The influenza repid test was negative. However, for the clinical suspicion of influenza or legionella, the specimens for servey of legionella and influenza were sent to the CDC. Intravenous dopamine was also given. Oral Tamiflu was added for suspected influenza viral infection since 3/3, and later, Influenza virus type A RNA PCR was positive from the CDC report. The chest CT on 3/3 showed consolidation with air-bronchogram at all 5 lobes of bilateral lungs (R't > L't)> Besides, honey-combing was also noted, with air cysts, bronchiectasis and interlobular septal thickening. Acute on chronic pneumonitits was favored. Underlying interstitial lung disesase such as usual intestitial pneumonia (UIP) / idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (IPF) or nonspecific interstitial pneumonia (NSIP) cannot be excluded. Because of severe dyspnea and hypoxic respiratory failure, intubation was done on 2016/3/3. Due to high fever, oral Levofloxacin was switched to IF Ciprofloxacin . On 3/7, because hypoxia was still noted under FiO2 100% (SpO2 around 85-90%), ECMO was inserted at 博仁醫院 and the patient was transferred to our 3C1 ICU on 3/7 for further care and treatment.
